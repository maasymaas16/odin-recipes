Honey Garlic Porkchops

Description

A super quick and easy porkchop recipe that will have your familty wanting seconds. A great way to prepare either bone-in or boneless porkchops, with an amazing honey-garlic taste!

Ingredients

  • 4 pork chops bone in or out
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minces
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat broiler or grill to medium-high heat. Season porck with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  2. Heat oil in a pan or skillet over medium high heat until hot. Sear chops on both sides until golden and cooked through (about 4-5 minutes each side). Transfer to a plate; set aside
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Melt butter in the same pan, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Saut√© garlic until fragrant (about 30 seconds). Add the honey, water and vinegar. Increase heat to medium-high and continue to cook until the sauce reduces down and thickens slightly (about 3-4 minutes), while stirring occasionally.
  4. Add pork back into the pan, baste generously with the sauce and broil/grill for 1-2 minutes, or until edges are slightly charred
  5. Serve right away and enjoy! Goes great with your favorite veggies!
